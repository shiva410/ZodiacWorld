About Us


Who are we and what is the purpose of this site?

Glad you are interested in learning about us! We are an organization devoted to astrology because we feel that it is a fun and interesting topic. Not only does it provide information about your personality, behavior, and general qualities, but astrology also provides similar information about your friends and families. Although it may not be taken entirely seriously, it is still a fun topic to learn about. We started this project this year since astrology seems to be becoming a widespread craze. Most importantly, we love it, and hope you will enjoy this site as well.

The purpose of this site is to explain the personality traits of each sign, as well as introduce users to new facts about their signs that they may not have previously known about. Please feel free to explore the resources tab if you wish to learn more about your sign.
